Seek First the Kingdom.

This is not a motivational page. It is a mandate page. The Kingdom is not an idea—it is government. When priority is corrected, provision follows.

Matthew 6:33 — “But seek first the kingdom of God and His righteousness, and all these things shall be added to you.”

Priority

The Kingdom is the first order, not the last option. A realignment of what governs your decisions.

Righteousness

Not public appearance. Right order. Right practices. Right weight placed on God’s instruction.

Addition

Provision becomes consequence, not pursuit. Added things follow aligned people.

The Kingdom Protocol

1
GOVERNMENT The Kingdom is rule. Decide what rules you: appetite, fear, culture—or Christ.
2
ORDER Seek first means sequence. Stop negotiating with God at the end of the day.
3
FRUIT “Added” is the evidence. Alignment produces outcomes you cannot manufacture.

One-line Hook

KINGDOM (Matt 6:33). A call to reorder life under God’s government—priority before provision.

A 7-Day Practice Plan

Not theory—alignment. Each day is one simple act of Kingdom priority.

Day 1

Write your current “first.” What receives your best time and attention?

Day 2

One hour of Scripture + silence. No phone. No noise. Only order.

Day 3

Righteousness audit: remove one habit that contradicts your confession.

Day 4

Serve someone quietly. No announcement. Kingdom is humility.

Day 5

Financial alignment: set aside a seed (giving/saving) with intention.

Day 6

Restore one relationship: apologize or forgive. Righteousness is repair.

Day 7

Write a “First Order” statement: what will govern you from today onward?

Kingdom Covenant

If you want this page to function as a movement entry point, this covenant becomes the signature line.

I choose to seek first the Kingdom of God and His righteousness.
I refuse a life governed by fear, appetite, or public approval.
I will reorder my decisions under God’s instruction—priority before provision.
